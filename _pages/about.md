Hi there! I am Yuhua Jiang, a PhD student in the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by [Prof. Qianchuan Zhao](https://www.au.tsinghua.edu.cn/info/1076/1610.html). I am also fortunate to collaborate with [Prof. Chongjie Zhang](https://mig-ai.github.io/person-zhangchongjie.html).

My research interests lie in reinforcement learning (RL), with a focus on exploration, self-play, LLM reasoning, and agentic RL. I am currently a research intern at ByteDance Seed, working on LLM reasoning and agentic RL with [Yufeng Yuan](https://openreview.net/profile?id=~Yufeng_Yuan1), [Yu Yue](https://openreview.net/profile?id=~YuYue2), and [Lin Yan](https://openreview.net/profile?id=~Lin_Yan1).
